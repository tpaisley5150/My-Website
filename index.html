Hi. I'm Tess.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in the rabbit hole of blogs & books, I can be found traveling, hiking and searching for new stories.

I was born and raised in Maine, where my love for the outdoors started from Maine's never ending forests and mountains. That constantly left me wanting to explore farther and wider.

One of my favorite passions, is storytelling. Whether its from reading, movies or even a video game. The magic of getting lost in a captivating story is what I live for.

Between my love for exploring and stories, I decided that I wanted to spend my life helping people bring the stories in their heads alive!
